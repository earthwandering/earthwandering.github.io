VicTownsend International Bike Loop Route

The VicTownsend International Bike Loop is a bicycle touring route that loops through the following places:

  • Port Townsend, Washington
  • Sequim, Washington
  • Port Angeles, Washington
  • Victoria, British Columbia
  • Sydney, British Columbia
  • The San Juan Islands, Washington
  • Anacortes, Washington
  • Oak Harbor, Washington
