    Wedding Celebration

    August 27th 2016

    Thank you everyone for having take part in our Wedding party last August 27th. It was wonderful to see everyone there, we had an amazing time!

    We would like to give a special thank to all our our amazing friends who helped us so much in making the party happen in a way even better than we thought was possible.

    Between helping gather crabs, setting up the stereo system, all of the additional dishes and liquor for the bar, building an awesome bean hole game, offers of places to stay to our families, the loan of additional stove and pots, picking up all sorts of last minute items, all of those who flew/drove very long distances (from Quebec, Ontario, Rhode Island, Connecticut and Japan), setting up the morning of the 27th and cleaning up the next day, taking the time to come celebrate with us and even more…
